Our Policies

Return Policy

If you're not satisfied with Codename Job, you can request a return within 30 days of purchase. All returns are processed through Stripe, our payment processor.

Refund Policy

We offer a full refund if requested within 30 days of purchase. After this period, refunds are provided on a prorated basis. Disputes can be filed through Stripe or by contacting our support team.

Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. No partial refunds are provided for mid-cycle cancellations.

Privacy

At Codename Job, we value your privacy and collect only the information necessary to enhance your job search experience. When you sign up for our Chrome extension, we gather your name, email address, job titles, and location to personalize job recommendations, facilitate communication, and tailor opportunities to your preferences. We do not collect any additional data beyond what you provide during signup. Your information is used solely to deliver and improve our services, and we are committed to safeguarding it in accordance with applicable laws.
