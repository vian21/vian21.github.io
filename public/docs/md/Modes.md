# Modes

The 7 Modes can be split into 2 categories: Major modes and minor modes.

1. Ionian - I
1. Dorian - II
1. Phrygian - III
1. Lydian - IV
1. Mixolydian - V
1. Aeolian - VI
1. Locrian - VII
