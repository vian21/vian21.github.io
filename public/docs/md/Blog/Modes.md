# Modes

The 7 Modes can be split into 2 categories: Major modes and minor modes.

1. Ionian - I
1. Dorian - II
1. Phrygian - III
1. Lydian - IV
1. Mixolydian - V
1. Aeolian - VI
1. Locrian - VII

## Major scales

### Ionian - The Major scale

| 1   | 2   | 3   | 4   | 5   | 6   | 7   |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

### Lydian - sharp 4

| 1   | 2   | 3   | `4#` | 5   | 6   | 7   |
| --- | --- | --- | ---- | --- | --- | --- |

### Mixolydian - flat 7

| 1   | 2   | 3   | 4   | 5   | 6   | `b7` |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | ---- |

## Minor scales

### Aeolian - Minor pentatonic

| 1   | 2   | b3  | 4   | 5   | b6  | b7  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

### Dorian - normal 6

| 1   | 2   | b3  | 4   | 5   | `6` | b7  |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

### Phrygian - flat 2

| 1   | `b2` | b3  | 4   | 5   | b6  | b7  |
| --- | ---- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

### Locrian - flat 2 and 5

| 1   | `b2` | b3  | 4   | `b5` | b6  | b7  |
| --- | ---- | --- | --- | ---- | --- | --- |

### [Superimposition](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7oKoiklz-WU)

- `Superimposition` is the technique of playing a pentatonic scale from a different key over a chord to evoke a differnt feel/mode

| Chord Type      | Superimposition (Minor Pentatonic) | Resulting Mode / Sound        |
| --------------- | ---------------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Major           | 3rd Degree                         | Ionian (adds 7th, 9th, 13th)  |
| Major           | Major 7th Degree                   | Lydian                        |
| Dominant        | 5th Degree                         | Mixolydian                    |
| Minor           | 5th Degree                         | Aeolian (adds 7th, 9th, 11th) |
| Minor           | 2nd Degree                         | Dorian                        |
| Minor           | Minor 7th Degree                   | Phrygian                      |
| Half-Diminished | Minor 3rd or Minor 7th Degree      | Locrian                       |
| Altered         | Minor 3rd Degree                   | Altered Scale Sound           |
